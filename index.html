About the Federation

The home of Turkmen ice hockey

The Turkmenistan Ice Hockey Federation (TKMIHF) is the national governing body responsible for organising, developing and promoting ice hockey throughout the country. We oversee the national teams, domestic competitions, coaching and officiating, and grassroots programmes that bring new players onto the ice.

Ice hockey in Turkmenistan has grown rapidly since the opening of modern indoor rinks in Ashgabat. Our mission is to build a sustainable pathway — from first skating lessons to international competition.
